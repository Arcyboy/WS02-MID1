Gtr's Specifications

The Nissan GT-R R35 Nismo has a 3.8-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine
that produces 600 HP and 481 lb-ft of torque. It can go from 0-60 MPH
in 2.5 seconds and reach a top speed of 205 MPH.
